About WETHIMA

Born in Dakar’s markets. Built for the people who keep them moving.

We are a Senegalese fintech building the missing layer between cash and mobile money — starting with the smallest, most painful transaction: change.

WETHIMA was born from a simple observation: in Senegal’s bustling markets, thousands of sales are lost every day because merchants don’t have small change. A customer wants to buy a 7,000 CFA item with a 10,000 note. The merchant has no 500s, no 1,000s. The sale walks away.

Founder Sokhna Ndiaye, a finance operations professional, saw this firsthand on her walks through Sandaga, Tilène, and Marché HLM. She watched women sell bags of attiéké for 500 CFA, then turn away three customers in a row because no one had coins. The problem wasn’t technology. Mobile money exists. The problem was the last 100 CFA — the friction at the end of the transaction.

She decided to build a solution that works with the way people really live and transact. Not a wallet that replaces cash. Not a bank account. A simple, honest tool that gives merchants instant micro-liquidity for the smallest, most overlooked transaction in the informal economy.

The WETHIMA ecosystem

Three pieces. One simple loop.

An app for the merchant. A wallet for the buyer. A kiosk in the market where cash and digital meet, face to face. Together, they form a closed loop designed for the way Senegal actually transacts.

Built around the market, not the office.

Each piece solves a real friction point observed in Dakar’s markets. None of them work alone — together, they create a system where digital change is instant, traceable, and convertible to cash at zero cost.

Designed with merchants, not for them.

01Merchant app

For Merchants — A daily credit line in your pocket.

A simple Android app (and USSD fallback) that gives you a daily credit line for giving digital change. Choose a monthly subscription plan and unlock instant liquidity up to 20,000 CFA. Every change you send is tracked in your personal ledger — no more guesswork at the end of the day.

  • Android app + USSD fallback for feature phones
  • Daily credit line up to 20,000 CFA
  • Automatic ledger of every change given
  • Weekly settlement at the kiosk
02Buyer wallet

For Buyers — A free wallet, with a welcome bonus.

A free wallet app. Receive change directly to your phone. Keep it as a digital balance to pay any WETHIMA merchant by scanning a QR code, withdraw it for free at the WETHIMA kiosk in the market, or just hold it for the next purchase.

  • 100 CFA welcome bonus on sign-up
  • Pay any WETHIMA merchant by QR code
  • Free cash-out at the WETHIMA kiosk
  • No balance minimums, no monthly fees

The Kiosk — Our trust anchor in the market.

A physical booth in the marketplace where merchants repay their credit, buyers cash out for free, and new users get onboarded. It’s our trust anchor — a place where digital money becomes real, and where the WETHIMA team is reachable, every working day.

  • Free cash-out for buyers
